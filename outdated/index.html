Test format reference
英検準1級 二次試験 / Eiken Pre-1 Speaking Test
Preparation
1 minute
Study the panels and topic
Narration
2 minutes
Describe all four panels in order
Questions
4 questions
No. 1 uses panel 4; Nos. 2–4 are general
Scoring
Pronunciation, vocabulary, grammar, content
Starting sentence
Always given — begin your narration with it exactly
Card flip
After No. 1, turn the card face-down for Nos. 2–4
Speaking tips
🗓
Use time markers
Connect panels with phrases like "Two weeks later," "As a result," and "However," to show how the story develops.
👁
Describe what you see
Point out characters' expressions and actions in each panel. This shows the examiner you are reading the pictures carefully.
💬
Give reasons
For discussion questions, always support your opinion with a reason. Use "because," "so," and "for example" to make your answer stronger.
⚖️
Consider both sides
For "Do you agree?" questions, it is fine to say "not completely." Showing you can think about different views often scores well.
🔄
Practise regularly
Use the 1-minute and 2-minute timers on every sheet to build confidence under real test conditions. Repeat until it feels natural.
📝
Review model answers
After answering, reveal the model and compare. Note vocabulary and sentence patterns you can borrow in your next practice session.
